Where We Started.

The North brand started in 1957 when Olympic gold medallist and trained engineer, Lowell North, thought to change the world of sailing through precision technology. Our founder fought in War World II, and as the war ended he started to concentrate in the sailing experinces. In the near 1984 when Kiteboarding was created by the Legaignoux brothers, Lowell bouth the projects for the Kites and Boards and aplied his great knowledge improving the products.
